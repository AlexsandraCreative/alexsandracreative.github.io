Our Mission

Mission Statement
NextGen EMT Scholarships was created to make EMT training accessible to passionate, service-driven individuals who want to protect and care for their communities. Every scholarship awarded helps someone step into a life-saving role.

Your Impact

Every year, dedicated individuals are turned away from life-saving careers simply because they can’t afford the training. With your support, we’re funding a future where every EMT student has a chance — regardless of finances.
